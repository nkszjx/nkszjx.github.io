Brief Information

I am currently a Post-Doc at Future Lab AI4EO, working with Prof. Xiao Xiang Zhu. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from IIR-TJU, Tianjin University, supervised by Prof. Jingyu Yang (杨敬钰). My research interests include Machine Learning/Deep Learning/Domain Adaptation, Urban Ecosystem Services, etc.
