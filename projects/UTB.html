The Urban Tree Canopy Cover dataset of Brazil

Jianhua Guo, Qingsong Xu, Yue Zen, Zhiheng Liu, Xiao Xiang Zhu

Study area

The Federative Republic of Brazil has a land area of 8.5 million km2 and a population of over 214 million. Fig.1 shows the specific location.

Workflow of this study

There are two mainstreams in our work. One stream generates urban mask products and the second stream generates tree canopy products.

Training Dataset

Urban tree canopy mapping dataset and Urban mask mapping dataset

Training Networks and Pytorch Codes

In this study, we used the same semi-supervised learning framework to train two CNN models for urban tree and mask segmentation, respectively. We use Deeplabv3+ (Chen et al., 2018) as our segmentation network. A standard binary classification network was designed as the discriminator in this semi-supervised adversarial learning framework. The pytorch code of this work is available from UTB_master
