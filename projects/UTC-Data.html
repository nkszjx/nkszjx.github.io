The Urban Tree Canopy Cover dataset of Brazil

Study area

The Federative Republic of Brazil has a land area of 8.5 million km2 and a population of over 214 million. Mainland of Brazil lies between latitudes 5◦N and 35◦S, and longitudes 35◦W and 74◦W. Fig.1 shows the specific location.

UrbanTree Brazil (UTB) dataset download

Using the proposed semi-supervised learning, we achieved novel nationwide urban tree canopy products covering 472 cities in Brazil. We named these urban tree canopy products the “UrbanTree Brazil (UTB)” dataset and made them available to the community (Brazil Urban Tree Canopy dataset ). This dataset include 26 states of Brazil (also includ Brasília).
